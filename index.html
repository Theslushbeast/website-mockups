Recommended Order

Start visual, then move toward trust-heavy services.

  1. Restaurant or cafe: easy to understand and visually appealing.
  2. Construction: project galleries make the value obvious.
  3. Clinic or wellness: shows trust, service clarity, and booking.
  4. Retail catalog: shows browsing, inquiries, and future e-commerce.
  5. Professional services: best once they understand the lead-generation pattern.

Simple Script

"These are sample websites built around common business types. They show how a website can bring in bookings, quote requests, visits, product inquiries, or consultations. If you imagine your logo, photos, services, and reviews in this layout, we can customize one for your business."

Customization Approach

Each demo becomes a real client site by changing the business details.

Brand

Replace logo, colors, typography, photography, and tone.

Offer

Swap in the client's services, products, menu, pricing, or packages.

Proof

Add reviews, licenses, team profiles, before-and-after work, or case results.

Conversion

Connect forms, booking tools, WhatsApp, maps, analytics, and email capture.
